Olympiads and other exams to appear for :


  1. NTSE :
    National Talent Search Examination (NTSE) is conducted every year for students of class 10th. The exams has two stages; the first stage is conducted by the state and the second stage is conducted by CBSE. The exams contains questions from physics, chemistry, maths, mental ability, biology, social studies and one language. All the students who qualify the second stage get a scholarhsip until they done with their studies.

  2. NSEJS :

  3. NSEP :
    National Standard Examination in Physics is conducted by IAPT. It is the stage 1 of the International Physics Olympiad. In the first stage, there are MCQs asked from the subject of Physics. All the students those who qualify the MI are eligible to appear for stage-2. There is a camp for the students who qualify stage-2. After the camp, 5 students are selected who represent India at IPO. You can appear for NSEP in any class.

  4. NSEC :
    National Standard Examination in Chemistry is conducted by IAPT.It is the first stage o International Chemistry Olympiad. In the first stage, there are MCQs asked from the subject of Chemistry. All the students those who qualify the MI are eligible to appear for stage-2. There is a camp for the students who qualify stage-2. After the camp, 5 students are selected who represent India at ICO. You can appear for NSEC in any class. The paper for all class is same.

  5. NSEA :
    National Standard Examination in Astronomy is conducted by IAPT.It is the first stage o International Astronomy Olympiad. In the first stage, there are MCQs asked from the subject of Astronomy and Mathematics. All the students those who qualify the MI are eligible to appear for stage-2. There is a camp for the students who qualify stage-2. After the camp, 5 students are selected who represent India at IAO. You can appear for NSEA in any class. There are two papers: one for class 12th students and another for all other students.

Engineering Entrance Exams for Undergraduate Courses


  1. IIT JEE (Mains):
    Previously known as AIEEE, IIT JEE (Mains) is conducted by NTA for admission to various B.E./B.Tech. courses in NITs, IIITs, IIEST and other GFTIs. Around 10 lakh students appear for the exam every year and only 2.5lac qualify for JEE (Advanced).
    Link to official website :jeemain.nic.in

  2. IIT JEE (Advanced):
    IIT JEE (Advanced) is conducted by IIT for admission into undergraduate B.E./B.Tech. courses at the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology. The candidates who qualify the JEE (Mains) examination are eligible to appear in this examination.
    Link to official website : jeeadv.nic.in

  3. BITSAT :
    BITSAT is conducted by BITS Pilani for admission into BITS Pilani, Dubai, Hyderabad and Goa campus. The exam contains 150 questions of Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English, Logical Reasoning. All the questions are single correct questions.
    Link to official website : bitsadmission.com

  4. IIIT-H UGEE :
    Indain Institute of Information Technology Hyderababd Undergraduate Entrance Exam is conducted by IIIT-H for admission into the institute. In the first round, an online aptitude test is conducted and few selected candidates are shortlisted for interview. IIIT-H offer admission into computer science and electronics course at the institute. There are total 90 seats for admission to the institute through this mode. The institute also offers admission through IIT JEE (Mains) exam. Through this mode, there are 150 seats for admission.
    Link to official website : iiit.ac.in

  5. VITEEE :
    VITEEE is conducted by VIT, Vellore for admission into B.E./B.Tech. courses at the three differemt campuses of VIT , i.e. Vellore, Chennai and Amravati. VIT was ranked the best private engineering institution in India by MHRD in NIRF Ranking 2019.
    Link to official website : vit.ac.in

  6. SRMJEEE :
    SRMJEEE is conducted by SRM, Ktr for admission into B.E./B.Tech courses at the three different campuses of SRM.
    Link to official website : srmuniv.ac.in
